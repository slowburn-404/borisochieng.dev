PP2 Graduation day

  1. EVENTS
  2. Graduation day

The Msingi School Academy would like to congratulate Pre Primary 2 class of 2022! This is a major milestone in your young lives and you should be incredibly proud of yourselves.

These little ones have come so far in just a few short years. They have learned to read and write, to count and to think critically. They have developed their social skills, learned to make friends and work together, and grown into confident, independent learners.

As we say goodbye to our PP2 graduands, we know that they are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. We have no doubt that they will continue to thrive and grow, and make us proud as they move on to the next stage of their education.

We are grateful to the teachers and staff who have nurtured and supported our graduands throughout their time in kindergarten. We are also grateful to the parents who have been a vital part of their children's education, and who have supported them every step of the way.

To our PP2 graduands, we say congratulations and good luck as you take this next big step in your educational journey. We know that you are ready to tackle whatever comes your way, and we can't wait to see all that you will accomplish in the future.
