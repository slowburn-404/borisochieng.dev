ABOUT US

The Msingi School Academy is founded from a Swahili word "Msingi" meaning foundation. The Msingi School Academy follows the Kenya National Competency Based Curriclum (CBC) integrated with learner centered approaches process.

Our Vision

To develop an innovative learning and teaching environment that helps children reach their full potential and foster teachers' professionalism

Our Mision

Our mission is to provide exceptional care to children while fostering each child's intellectual, social, physical and moral development through play and collaboration

Our Facilities

The school has an enabling and conducive environment for all to learn and well equiped with:

 Children friendly playing facilities

 Adequate playing space

 Child friendly library

 Well equiped classes and play areas

 Transport
