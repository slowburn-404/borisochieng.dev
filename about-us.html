ABOUT US

Here is our story

The Msingi School Academy is founded from a Swahili word "Msingi" meaning foundation. The Msingi School Academy follows the Kenya National Competency Based Curriclum (CBC) integrated with learner centered approaches process.

Our Vision

To develop an innovative learning and teaching environment that helps children reach their full potential and foster teachers' professionalism

Our Mision

To provide exceptional care to children while fostering each child's intellectual, social, physical and moral development through play and collaboration

Activities we offer

Dance

A school where children can learn dance classes in a friendly environment.

Music

A school where children are taught music classes, play by leaving and joining the music. This is a fun way to stay active. It doesn't matter what skill level you have, as long as you want to stay active then we can have fun together!

Chess

We believe that chess helps to build confidence and self-esteem, which can lead to better test scores within schools. We teach kids chess, and we encourage their parents to learn too.

Swimming

A school where children are taught swimming classes and water sports. The teachers at this school will understand the importance of swimming in your child's development, and will teach them how to swim correctly for their age.

Creative Arts

Art is an incredible way to learn and grow, and we believe that every child has a unique set of skills waiting to develop. Together, we create a safe environment where kids feel comfortable sharing their ideas.

Golf

If you want to teach your child about golf, then this school is the best place for your child to learn.
